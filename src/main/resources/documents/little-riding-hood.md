# 🧺 Little Red Riding Hood

Once upon a time, in a small village near a deep forest, there lived a sweet little girl who was loved by everyone. Her grandmother gave her a beautiful red hooded cloak, and she wore it so often that everyone called her **Little Red Riding Hood**.

## 🌲 A Walk Through the Forest

One day, Little Red Riding Hood’s mother asked her to take a basket of food to her sick grandmother who lived on the other side of the forest.

> “Do not talk to strangers, and do not stray from the path,” her mother warned.

Little Red Riding Hood promised to be careful and set off happily into the forest.

## 🐺 The Big Bad Wolf

As she walked, she met a sly wolf.

> “Good morning, little girl. Where are you going?” asked the wolf.

Little Red Riding Hood, forgetting her mother’s advice, told him about her grandmother and where she lived. The wolf quickly ran ahead, planning a wicked trick.

## 🏠 Grandmother’s Cottage

The wolf reached the grandmother’s house first, knocked on the door, and pretended to be Little Red Riding Hood. Once inside, he locked the grandmother away and dressed in her clothes before climbing into her bed.

Soon after, Little Red Riding Hood arrived and noticed something strange.

> “Grandmother, what big eyes you have!”  
> “All the better to see you with,” replied the wolf.

> “What big teeth you have!”  
> “All the better to eat you with!”

And with that, the wolf leapt from the bed!

## 🪓 A Brave Rescue

Just in time, a woodsman passing by heard the noise and rushed inside. He scared away the wolf and rescued both Little Red Riding Hood and her grandmother.

They were safe at last.

## 🌈 The Lesson

Little Red Riding Hood learned an important lesson that day:

**Always listen to good advice and never talk to strangers.**

And they all lived happily ever after.
